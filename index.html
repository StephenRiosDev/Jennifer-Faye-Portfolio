About

I live in the Twin Cities of Minnesota. I'm a full stack web developer with a background in project management and leadership.

I am a creative problem solver, obsessively detail-oriented, and enjoy troubleshooting. My brain works best at the intersection of creative and analytical, a place I've found to be very condusive to website development. I really like the challenging, yet satisfying world of coding.
